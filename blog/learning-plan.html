Learning Plan

Throughout history civilisations have continued to evolve. Those who evolve with it thrive and those who do not will get left behind. Take John Deere for example. John Deere was born in 1804, the same year the locomotive was created. He witnessed the locomotives growth through the years and identified areas that could use this same system where he could capitalise its abilities. In 1837 John Deere created the first self-scouring steel plow. His legacy grew from there.

So too have I seen the growth of the internet and the "age of information" and its transition into the "age of intelligence".

Long Term Pathway

Earlier this year I decided to join the army but the corps was not willing to let me leave so easily. It would seem that I had developed an interesting and very niche set of skills over the years that make it hard for me to just leave. Because of this I decided to create a system to replace people with my skills and increase battlefield survivability.

Strengths and Limitations

When it comes to learning I love to explore new ideas. This allows me to find out why things work and identify the algorithms. However, this also can prove a pitfall as I tend to end up down rabbit holes and soak up more time than necessary. Because of this I am sicking to the time-box as best I can when conducting the lessons but creating a note to follow up on information outside of these lessons.

Skills I'd like to develop

I tend to approach each learning experience with and open mind. I would like to stick to the time box suggestions and stay out of the rabbit holes ass much as possible.

Commitments

I would like to make a commitment to my peers that I will push to understand them and where their thought processes stem from. This should help us flow better during group learning sessions.

I would like to make a commitment to my peers and facilitators that I will reach out for help when I am stuck and that I will do my best to assist my peers when they are stuck. I will try to understand and solve the problem by myself for 15min. I will then search the internet for 5 min before reaching out on Discord.

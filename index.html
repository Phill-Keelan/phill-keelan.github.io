Throughout history civilisations have continued to evolve. Those who evolve with it thrive and those who do not will get left behind. Take John Deere for example. John Deere was born in 1804, the same year the locomotive was created. He witnessed the locomotives growth through the years and identified areas that could use this same system where he could capitalise its abilities. In 1837 John Deere created the first self-scouring steel plow. His legacy grew from there.
